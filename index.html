Screen to Shelf

I design things
that get made.

I currently handle print production start to finish — business cards, stickers, die-cut packaging — and I don't just hand off a file. I take it through to the finished physical product myself. That's given me something a lot of designers don't have: I know how a design actually holds up once it leaves the screen.

I also work with sublimation printing on fabric, mugs, and other surfaces, and do engraving as well — so I design for materials, not just for a canvas in Illustrator.

Based in Dubai Hills, the clients I deal with day to day come from all over — different nationalities, different kinds of businesses, different expectations. It's taught me to read a brief fast and adapt without losing the idea behind it.
